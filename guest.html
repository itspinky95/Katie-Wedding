Accommodation

There are lots of hotels and bnbs in Harrogate town centre. Weekends can be expensive so we recommend booking in advance. For the most reasonable prices we recommend having a look on Airbnb. Anything in the town centre is walking distance from the Sun Pavillion but these are limited. The link below shows properties available on the 3rd of September with free parking.

Top Harrogate Places to Stay & Holiday Rentals with Free parking on premises | Airbnb® - Airbnb

There are 2 travel lodge hotels available in the city centre. We would recommend West Park over Harrogate centre. It is still only around a 15 minute walk from the Sun Pavillion and the one in the centre of Harrogate is on the same street as all the bars so could be loud through the night.

The Crown Hotel offers a discount using the code ‘BESTBOOKDIRECT’ of 12.5% for wedding guests of ceremonies at the Sun Pavillion and is one of the closest Hotels to the Sun Pavillion: 4 -star hotel in Harrogate |The Crown Hotel
